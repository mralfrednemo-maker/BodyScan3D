  

## 1. Definition of Done framing

This Definition of Done is a **contract-enforcement bar** for a self-hosted, phone-first body-part capture system. The system is done only when it can take ordinary non-expert capture under real clinic/studio conditions and produce a **workflow-alive**, append-only asset family that supports honest viewing, stable placement where authority exists, bounded preview where authority exists, deterministic replay/regeneration, and publishability without overclaiming. A visually impressive but workflow-dead result is not done. Honest partial states are valid when they remain explicit; hidden uncertainty is not.  

## 2. Ground rules inherited from the approved design

The following are binding:

* one accepted capture yields one canonical asset family under stable identity and append-only lineage
* the closed capability set is fixed: photoreal viewing, surface-anchored placement, deformable preview, provenance/regeneration, metadata/confidence signaling
* region-level truth beats summaries
* honest partial publication is allowed; a hidden second processability gate is forbidden
* patch is repair, never silent rewrite
* no fake watertightness, fake continuity, fake metric trust, or clinical/diagnostic/predictive implication by default
* backend complexity must not leak backward onto the operator
* capture, processing, storage, and serving must be self-hosted on operator-controlled infrastructure.   

## 3. Whole-system Definition of Done

The whole product is done only when all of the following are true:

**Product-level**

* non-expert operators complete a standard body-part capture on first attempt in at least 4/5 sessions after at most 10 minutes of onboarding
* median active capture time is at most 45 seconds; p95 at most 90 seconds
* at least 95% of completed captures yield a processed asset without manual rescue under normal conditions
* end-to-end capture → upload → processing → usable asset completes in at least 95% of attempts on supported devices and infrastructure
* imperfect captures degrade to flagged-but-usable outputs wherever physically possible instead of hard-failing.  

**Output-contract**

* every accepted session produces, or can deterministically regenerate, the required capability stack
* cross-output spatial consistency holds on claimed regions
* reopen/save/re-derive must not silently move tattoos, change preview meaning, or break correspondence
* machine-readable coverage/confidence/flagged-region outputs must exist and remain consumable downstream.  

**Lineage / privacy / durability**

* stage versions, QC outputs, and publish manifests are never mutated in place
* there is no runtime dependency on external reconstruction APIs, license servers, or hosted QC services
* raw and derived artifacts are encrypted at rest and in transit
* access is role-scoped and audit-logged
* purge, denial, publish, stale/rebind, and supersession events are recorded in append-only logs
* telemetry is opt-in, operational, and excludes imagery or identifying payloads. 

## 4. Subsystem DoD — Capture UX and shot-taking

Capture UX is done when capture is calm, silent-by-default, non-gatekeeping, and centered on **processable-enough** capture rather than ideal capture.

**Must exist**

* M1 video sweep as the primary visible mode
* bounded M2 fallback
* bounded patch capture offer
* exactly the approved four review outcomes: usable, usable with flagged regions, worth a short patch, retry recommended
* downstream-visible review outcome context in mobile provenance.  

**Must be true**

* median zero corrective interventions, p95 at most one soft nudge, hard ceiling three interventions
* no hidden expertise requirement
* M1 remains the dominant successful path
* retry remains rare and reserved for below-floor cases
* patch is bounded recovery, not a third dominant mode.  

**Non-go**

* intervention inflation
* retake inflation
* mode-switch nagging
* silent hard-gating
* “surveillance/proctored” feel
* success achieved mainly by tightening operator choreography. 

## 5. Subsystem DoD — Mobile capture pipeline

Mobile is done when it captures raw media plus synchronized provenance and delivers an integrity-verified session bundle under one immutable `canonical_session_id`.

**Required artifacts**

* session manifest
* ordered media inventory
* per-sample metadata streams
* per-file hashes and bundle-level integrity proof / Merkle agreement
* resumable upload state
* backend durable-persistence acknowledgment.  

**Required behaviors**

* no destructive downsampling, beauty filtering, or hidden frame filtering before verified upload
* interrupted uploads resume cleanly
* upload state survives backgrounding and recoverable interruption
* no transient failure loses raw capture data
* local privacy enforcement is maintained.  

**Non-go**

* plaintext media leakage
* transcode replacing original media without provable continuity
* upload requires manual restart
* external runtime dependency
* incomplete/corrupt session bundle declared successful.  

## 6. Subsystem DoD — FSCQI

FSCQI is done when it produces an honest, reconstruction-ready observation package without becoming a hidden perfectionist gate.

**Required artifacts**

* curated primary tier
* extended candidate tier
* raw reference map
* dual-level coverage descriptor
* weak-region register
* capture-health summary
* machine-readable provenance
* `fscqi_bundle_version`
* explicit four-state processability verdict. 

**Required behaviors**

* unique moderate-quality views can outrank pristine duplicates
* minor blur, exposure drift, and coverage gaps do not force retry
* partial captures can pass through as `PROCESS_WITH_FLAGS`
* downstream sees the weak-region truth instead of a scalar simplification. 

**Non-go**

* scalar utility used as a hidden veto
* below-perfect capture treated as failure by default
* weak-region signals dropped before downstream use
* retry inflation from ordinary handheld messiness. 

## 7. Subsystem DoD — SIAT

SIAT is done when target anatomy is isolated honestly, with retained context margins and ambiguity preserved where evidence is weak.

**Required artifacts**

* `target_alpha_soft`
* `target_core_mask`
* `target_mask_hard`
* `static_rigid_core` / `pose_safe_support_mask`
* `boundary_confidence_channel`
* ambiguity tags and occlusion labels. 

**Required behaviors**

* no zero-shot fragility
* no forced binary certainty under uncertainty
* retained target-adjacent context sufficient for placement/preview downstream
* hard exclusion reserved for clear non-target evidence. 

**Non-go**

* crisp boundary without confidence
* silent target-identity drift
* aggressive context stripping that starves downstream workflows. 

## 8. Subsystem DoD — REG

REG is done when it yields the strongest honest multi-view registration state available, including partial/fragmented graphs, and establishes a canonical body-part pose frame without hallucinating measurement trust.

**Required artifacts**

* per-observation intrinsics/extrinsics
* `registration_state`
* session registration graph
* `pose_version`
* scale regime and scale confidence band
* `metric_trust_allowed`
* `measurement_validity_claim`
* `measurement_use_prohibited` where applicable
* `feature_support_regime ∈ {core_only, core_plus_context, fallback_wide}`.  

**Required behaviors**

* partial connectedness is allowed
* weak support remains explicit
* scale posture is honest and machine-readable
* “close but slightly shifted” is failure, not tolerance.  

**Non-go**

* forcing a single global component from unearned evidence
* hallucinated scale confidence
* downstream metric or clinical implication without validated support. 

## 9. Subsystem DoD — DG

DG is done when it produces honest geometry that preserves fragments, open boundaries, usefulness zones, and severe concerns instead of cosmetically closing gaps.

**Required artifacts**

* `geometry_version`
* `surface_fragment_set`
* `hole_and_open_boundary_map`
* `surface_usefulness_zones`
* structural proxy and appearance scaffold as distinct geometric/appearance responsibilities
* explicit open-boundary semantics.  

**Required behaviors**

* fragment-preserving output
* open boundaries remain explicit
* zero placement-relevant anchor zones triggers `severe_geometry_concern`
* unsupported gaps are not bridged for cosmetic neatness. 

**Non-go**

* hallucinated skin closure
* unsupported fragment merging
* suppressing coherent partial surfaces just because 360° coverage was not achieved. 

## 10. Subsystem DoD — Photoreal view-capable realization

This subsystem is done when it mints a lineage-addressable `view_version` that fulfills the viewing contract for the regions and states it claims.

**Required artifacts**

* view bundle
* `view_version`
* pipeline/version fingerprint
* explicit link to the asset family and lineage tuple
* either full photoreal route or explicit appearance-only viewing route when that is the highest honest route available. 

**Required behaviors**

* photo-like output on near-capture viewpoints
* believable skin tone, microtexture, and lighting plausibility
* outside the supported envelope, degrade with blur/ghosting rather than tears, floaters, inversions, or collapse
* appearance does not confer edit authority.  

**Non-go**

* beautiful orbit but workflow-dead asset
* hidden randomness that changes practical output
* view-only output presented as sufficient for persistent editing
* material photoreal change hidden under another version axis. 

## 11. Subsystem DoD — EDSIM

EDSIM is done when it converts imperfect upstream substrate into bounded persistent authority where support exists, and explicit guarded / refused / appearance-only / stale states where support does not.

**Required artifacts**

* `anchor_chart_manifest.json`
* `placement_authority_map.json`
* `preview_authority_map.json`
* `appearance_only_routes.json`
* `edit_region_definitions.json`
* `placement_state_bundles.jsonl`
* `preview_edit_state_bundles.jsonl`
* `placement_on_preview_bindings.jsonl`
* `stale_rebind_register.json`
* `before_after_lineage.json`
* `edit_readiness_summary.json`
* refusal-zone files
* decision/admission logs.  

**Required behaviors**

* placement is persistent only on real anchor domains
* preview persistence exists only for `preview_authoritative` and `preview_guarded`
* `preview_appearance_only` routes are non-persistent by construction
* placement and preview bundles are orthogonal and append-only
* `auto_rebind_identical` only for identical meaning; otherwise `stale_requires_review` or `retired`
* edit-readiness summary is descriptive only and may not override region-level maps.  

**Non-go**

* edits persisted over unsupported substrate
* silent relocation or silent parameter mutation
* appearance-only promoted into persistent authority
* upstream geometry/pose/isolation truth overwritten by EDSIM.  

## 12. Subsystem DoD — OQSP

OQSP is done when it organizes, validates, fuses, classifies, and publishes without re-adjudicating upstream truth.

**Required artifacts**

* immutable `publish_manifest.json`
* 8-artifact QC set including asset/QC manifest, capability readiness, severe concern aggregation, and related integrity/conflict surfaces
* lineage-aware artifact refs
* `publishability_class`
* append-only state transition records
* immutable parent pointers and pipeline fingerprints.   

**Required behaviors**

* durable resumable staged workflow
* dependency-scoped invalidation after patch, not blind full rerun
* preserve upstream weakness/refusal/stale signals rather than flattening them
* layered publishing, not binary publishing
* no stronger geometry, confidence, edit authority, or metric trust may be invented downstream
* external publish is forbidden unless an honest view-capable route exists.  

**Non-go**

* flattening refusal/guarded maps into a cleaner headline
* minting publish from failed-unrecoverable lineage
* unresolved hash/parent-pointer contradictions
* QC honesty channels reduced to decorative prose.  

## 13. Cross-cutting Definition of Done

**Stable identity and lineage**

* one canonical capture-origin identity binds 1:1 to one canonical asset-family identity
* descendants are append-only
* no version, publish manifest, or QC output is mutated in place.  

**Patch / stale / rebind**

* patch is repair, never silent rewrite
* identical-only automatic rebind
* non-identical downstream meaning becomes stale or retired
* no silent remapping of tattoos or preview semantics.  

**Measurement / scale honesty**

* `metric_trust_allowed` defaults false unless explicitly earned upstream
* scale regime and measurement posture propagate unchanged
* no clinical/metrological claim by default.  

**Confidence / flagged-region integrity**

* coverage, confidence, and flagged-region outputs are machine-readable
* flagged regions propagate across appearance, placement, preview, and publish layers
* summary classes never override per-region truth.  

**Deterministic regeneration**

* same canonical asset + same compatible derivation context + same parameters = same practical output
* “close but slightly shifted” is failure
* pipeline changes mint explicit new descendants.  

**Purge / regeneration truth**

* purge must be explicit, auditable, lineage-safe, and cascade-safe
* after purge removes required provenance, the asset must no longer present itself as regenerable
* purge may end capability; it may not create a false claim of replayability/regenerability.   

**Operational telemetry tripwires**

* intervention counters, retry inflation, patch inflation, and M1/M2 ratio must be monitored continuously
* telemetry must detect backend strictness leaking into operator burden
* telemetry state must not change capture or upload behavior.  

## 14. Release and publish gates

**Minimum internally valid state**

* verified identity and lineage
* machine-readable honesty channels intact
* structural and/or edit-capable substrate may exist even without external publishability
* partial, guarded, appearance-only, and refusal-bearing states may remain internally valid if truth surfaces are preserved. This majority synthesis explicitly rejects the losing rule that all outputs must be non-empty before internal validity can exist.   

**Minimum externally publishable state**

* an honest view-capable route exists
* publication preserves machine-readable bounds and flags
* appearance-only viewing may satisfy the external publish floor when it is the highest honest route
* structural-only or edit-only states may remain valid internal lineage without external publish.  

**Edit-capable publish floor**

* view-capable floor satisfied
* at least one honestly supported placement and/or bounded preview region exists
* region bounds, authority class, and refusal zones remain exposed
* publication does not imply stronger authority outside those regions.  

**Blocks publishing**

* missing honesty channels
* lineage corruption / missing parent pointers
* unresolved cryptographic integrity failures
* failed-unrecoverable branch attempting to publish
* no honest view-capable route
* silent mutation, false metric trust, or uncertainty laundering.  

## 15. Required evidence and verification matrix

The system cannot claim done without evidence for:

* capture usability bars and intervention-frequency telemetry
* upload resume/persistence/integrity tests
* processability verdict correctness and weak-region propagation tests
* SIAT ambiguity-preservation and occlusion-tag tests
* REG partial-graph and scale-honesty tests
* DG fragment-preservation and open-boundary honesty tests
* photo-like viewing tests on near-trajectory viewpoints
* placement replay tests
* preview replay tests
* cross-output consistency checks
* patch regression checks
* stale/rebind behavior checks
* lineage integrity checks across `fscqi_bundle_version`, `isolation_version`, `pose_version`, `geometry_version`, `view_version`, and `edit_sim_version`
* publish-manifest and QC artifact presence/integrity checks
* purge/regeneration truth tests
* telemetry tripwire regression tests for calm capture.    

## 16. Release-blocking non-go conditions

Release is blocked by any of the following:

* gatekeeping capture
* photo-mode creep
* fragile video pipeline
* workflow-dead outputs
* self-hosting in name only
* silent overclaiming
* capture-side shifting of backend responsibility
* asset identity drift
* hidden expertise gate
* ideal-conditions-only success
* external publish without honest view-capable route
* false watertightness, false continuity, or false metric trust
* persistence of edits over unsupported substrate
* publish from failed-unrecoverable lineage
* purge that leaves a false regeneration claim intact.  

## 17. Residual implementation risks that are NOT design gaps

These remain real risks even if the DoD is implemented correctly:

* whether short handheld RGB capture is sufficient often enough across the target body-part set
* whether photoreal view, stable anchoring, and bounded preview can stay in visually-undetectable lockstep on weakly featured skin
* whether identical-only stale/rebind policy creates too much review friction
* whether preview remains photo-like near weak/open boundaries often enough to stay product-useful
* whether confidence and weak-region foresight are calibrated usefully without becoming over-conservative
* whether self-hosted minutes-not-hours latency remains feasible on difficult sessions and long lineage chains
* whether telemetry tripwires are sensitive enough to catch capture-regression early without becoming noise.  

**Not clear enough / not feasible to harden further from the current majority record**

* an exact numeric cross-output drift budget
* any required percentage floor for `PROCESS_CLEAN` or unflagged assets
* an exact legal retention duration policy
* exact downstream UI mechanics beyond the obligation to honor machine-readable authority/refusal signals.  

## 18. Final integrated Definition of Done summary

The system is done only when it can honestly turn calm, phone-first capture into one canonical, append-only, self-hosted asset family that is **workflow-alive**: view-capable, placement-capable where authority exists, preview-capable where authority exists, replayable, regenerable while provenance exists, privacy-safe, and publishable only within explicit authority/refusal/guarded/stale/appearance-only/measurement boundaries. Honest partial success is valid. Silent mutation, silent overclaim, fake continuity, fake metric trust, hidden second gating, and operator-burden backflow are not.
